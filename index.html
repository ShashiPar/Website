Welcome to Maharashtra

Maharashtra, a state spanning west-central India, is best known for its fast-paced capital, Mumbai (formerly Bombay). This sprawling metropolis is the seat of the Bollywood film industry. Maharashtra is also home to the ancient Ellora and Ajanta caves, which are adorned with Buddhist shrines, monasteries, and temples. The state is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and beautiful landscapes.

Mumbai

Mumbai, the capital city of Maharashtra, is known for its bustling streets, vibrant culture, and historical landmarks. Must-visit places include the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and the iconic Taj Mahal Palace Hotel.

Pune

Pune, often referred to as the "Oxford of the East," is known for its educational institutions and vibrant nightlife. Key attractions include Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, and the Osho Ashram.

Nashik

Nashik is a city with rich religious and historical significance, famous for its vineyards and the Kumbh Mela. Major sites include the Trimbakeshwar Temple, Sula Vineyards, and Panchavati.

Aurangabad

Aurangabad is renowned for its historical monuments and cultural heritage, including the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Ajanta and Ellora Caves. Other notable spots include Bibi Ka Maqbara and Daulatabad Fort.

Nagpur

Nagpur, known as the "Orange City," is famous for its oranges and the Deekshabhoomi, a significant Buddhist monument. Important places to visit are the Nagpur Central Museum, Sitabuldi Fort, and Ramtek Temple.
